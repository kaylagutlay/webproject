Leche Flan

Leche flan is the silky-smooth superstar of Filipino desserts! It's like a sweet golden treasure made of creamy caramel and egg custard that melts in your mouth with every bite. Always the highlight of fiestas, birthdays, or even random Sundays, leche flan never fails to steal the spotlight. It's the dessert that makes you say, "Ang sarap, isa pa nga!" Whether it's served on its own or as the crown of your halo-halo, leche flan is the ultimate comfort food that feels like a warm hug from lola.

Ingredients:

For the Caramel:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

For the Custard:

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 1 can (300ml) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (370ml) evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Recipe:

  1. Prepare the Caramel:
    • In a saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar and water.
    • Cook while swirling the pan (don’t stir!) until the mixture turns into a golden caramel.
    • Quickly pour the caramel into llanera (flan molds) or a baking dish, tilting to coat the bottom evenly. Let it set.
  2. Make the Custard:
    • In a mixing bowl, whisk egg yolks gently until smooth (avoid creating bubbles).
    • Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined but not frothy.
    • Strain the mixture through a fine sieve to remove any lumps for a silky texture.
  3. Assemble the Flan:
    • Pour the custard mixture into the caramel-lined molds, filling them about 3/4 full.
  4. Cook the Flan
    • Steam Method: Cover the molds with foil and steam over low heat for 30–40 minutes, or until the flan is set.
    • Oven Method: Cover the molds with foil and place them in a water bath (bain-marie). Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 45–50 minutes.
  5. Cool and Serve:
    • Let the flan cool completely before refrigerating for at least 2 hours.
    • To serve, run a knife along the edges of the mold, then invert onto a plate to release the caramel.

Leche flan is perfect for fiestas, celebrations, or when you need a creamy, sweet indulgence!
