About Us

"Welcome to Tam-Is PINOY – where tradition meets taste and culture comes alive in every click. Our website is dedicated to showcasing the rich and vibrant flavors of the Philippines, celebrating the beloved sweet delicacies that have brought joy to generations. From classic recipes passed down through time to modern twists on old favorites, we aim to share the heart and soul of Filipino cuisine with you. Each delicacy we feature carries a story, a memory, and a connection to our roots, reminding us of home and the warmth of togetherness that food brings.

But *Tam-Is PINOY* is more than just a recipe site; it’s a journey into the rich heritage of the Philippines. Explore the history and traditions behind each dish, connect with our mission to preserve and share Filipino culture, and discover tips and tricks to recreate authentic flavors in your own kitchen. Whether you're a seasoned cook, a curious foodie, or someone looking to reconnect with their Filipino identity, *Tam-Is PINOY* is your gateway to savoring not just the taste but the heart of the Philippines. Join us, and let’s keep the sweetness of Filipino culture alive!"

Our Mission

"Our mission at Tam-Is PINOY is to rekindle the love and appreciation for beloved Filipino sweet delicacies—foods that bring joy, memories, and deep connections to our hearts. Through every recipe, story, and tradition we share, we aim to pass on the sweetness of Filipino culture to future generations, ensuring it continues to be cherished and celebrated wherever in the world.

We strive to be a bridge for Filipinos yearning to connect with their roots and for those eager to discover the richness of our cuisine. At *Tam-Is PINOY*, it’s not just about food—it’s about sharing love, unity, and pride in our Filipino identity. Together, let’s continue to celebrate and embrace the dishes that warm our hearts."

Our Journey to Tam-Is PINOY: A Dream to Promote Filipino Sweet Delicacies

As 3rd-year BSIT students from Sorsogon State University - Bulan Campus, we've always believed that technology has the power to connect people, preserve culture, and inspire change. Growing up in a country with such a rich culinary tradition, we realized that one aspect of our heritage stood out among the rest — Filipino sweet delicacies. These beloved treats, passed down through generations, are more than just food; they are a testament to the creativity, warmth, and resilience of the Filipino spirit.

The idea for Tam-Is PINOY was born during one of our brainstorming sessions. We wanted to create something that went beyond grades and projects — something meaningful, something that could have a lasting impact. We thought, “What if we could build a platform to showcase and promote the sweet delicacies that Filipinos have loved for decades?” These delicacies hold memories for families, celebrations, and traditions. And so, with a shared vision, we embarked on a journey to bring this dream to life.

Turning Passion into Purpose

Creating Tam-Is PINOY was no easy feat. Balancing our responsibilities as students with designing and coding the website was both challenging and rewarding. Late nights spent debugging code, long hours researching Filipino desserts, and brainstorming creative ways to present them tested our patience and determination. But through it all, we were fueled by a common goal: to promote and preserve a piece of Filipino heritage in the digital age.

We envisioned Tam-Is PINOY as more than just a website; it's a celebration of our culture. Through every line of code and every page design, we poured our hearts into making sure the platform reflected the vibrant and colorful spirit of the Philippines. Beyond that, we wanted to use our skills to give back to local communities by creating awareness about these delicacies, helping small-scale Filipino vendors gain recognition, and encouraging future generations to appreciate our unique culinary identity.

Now, as we look at the finished product, we're not just proud of what we've built — we're inspired by the journey it represents. It's a story of perseverance, teamwork, and passion. Tam-Is PINOY is more than a website. It's a symbol of our love for our country, our dedication to our craft, and our hope to inspire others to embrace and promote what makes us uniquely Filipino.
